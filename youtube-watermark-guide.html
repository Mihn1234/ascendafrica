📋 Watermark Specifications

🎯 Technical Requirements:

  • Dimensions: Exactly 150 x 150 pixels
  • File Format: PNG (with transparency)
  • File Size: Under 1MB
  • Background: Transparent for overlay
  • Design: High contrast for visibility
💡 Pro Tip: The watermark appears as a clickable subscribe button for viewers who aren't subscribed to your channel. When clicked, it opens a subscription dialog, making it easy for viewers to subscribe without leaving your video.

🚀 How to Add Your Watermark

📺 Upload to YouTube Studio:

  1. Go to YouTube Studio (studio.youtube.com)
  2. Click "Customization" in the left sidebar
  3. Select "Branding" tab
  4. Scroll to "Video Watermark" section
  5. Click "Upload" and select your downloaded PNG file
  6. Choose display timing:
    • End of video (recommended for subscribe prompts)
    • Custom start time (e.g., after intro)
    • Entire video (for brand protection)
  7. Click "Save" and then "Publish"

⚙️ Recommended Settings:

  1. Display Time: "End of video" - appears in last 15 seconds
  2. Purpose: Subscribe button for non-subscribers
  3. Position: Bottom-right corner (automatic)
  4. Size: Scales automatically based on player size
